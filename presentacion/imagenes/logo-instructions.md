# Company Logo Instructions

To add your company logo to the presentation:

1. Place your company logo file in the `imagenes/` folder
2. Name it `company-logo.png` 
3. Recommended specifications:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - Size: 200x200 pixels or higher for better quality
   - Aspect ratio: Square or slightly rectangular

The logo will appear:
- In the title slide next to the author name
- In the bottom-right corner of all slides

If you don't have a company logo, you can:
- Remove the logo lines from the LaTeX file
- Use a generic icon from FontAwesome5
- Create a simple text-based logo
